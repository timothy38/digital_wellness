Protect Your Digital Life: Practice Digital Wellness & Cyber Safety

Take charge of your screen time, secure your data, and live better online.

Why It Matters

In today’s connected world, we spend more time online than ever before — chatting, studying, scrolling, and streaming. But are we doing it safely? Are we mentally healthy? This site will help you explore smart, balanced, and secure ways to live online.

Digital wellness is not about completely avoiding technology, but about making intentional choices to use it in ways that support rather than disrupt our lives. Cyber safety ensures our personal data and privacy are protected as we navigate the internet, social media, and digital platforms.

Key Focus Areas

Digital Wellness

Maintain your mental and physical health by setting boundaries, taking breaks, and using technology mindfully to enhance your well-being.

Cyber Safety

Social Media Awareness

Social media can connect us, inspire us, and inform us. However, it can also lead to comparison, anxiety, or misinformation. Learn to curate your feed, manage your time, and critically evaluate content.

Healthy Online Gaming

Gaming can be a fun and engaging hobby, but overplaying can lead to neglecting real-life responsibilities. Set time limits, avoid toxic interactions, and explore games that promote problem-solving and creativity.

Managing Privacy Settings

Every app you use has privacy settings, but do you know how to use them? Regularly review what data you share, limit public access, and be cautious with location-sharing and permissions.
